# GEHU Bhimtal Campus – Hostel & Mess Fees (2024–2025)

All hostels at GEHU Bhimtal are **Non-AC** and available for both boys and girls. Fees vary based on room type.

## Hostel Room Types and Fees

| Room Type        | Hostel & Mess Fee | Security Fee | Laundry Fee | Total (Approx.) |
|------------------|-------------------|--------------|-------------|-----------------|
| 4-Seater Room    | ₹85,000           | ₹5,000       | ₹4,750       | ₹94,750         |
| 3-Seater Room    | ₹75,000           | ₹5,000       | ₹4,750       | ₹84,750         |
| 2-Seater Room    | ₹95,000           | ₹5,000       | ₹4,750       | ₹1,04,750       |

## Notes:
- **Laundry charges** of ₹4,750 are not included in the hostel & mess fees.
- Hostel security deposit is **₹5,000** (refundable).
- All hostels are on a **Non-AC** basis.
