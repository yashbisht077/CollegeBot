[Graphic Era (Deemed to be University)]
- Recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) under Section 3 of the UGC Act, 1956.
- Accredited with an A+ grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC).
- Programs approved by regulatory bodies such as:
  - All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE)
  - National Board of Accreditation (NBA)
  - Pharmacy Council of India (PCI)
  - Bar Council of India (BCI), where applicable

[Graphic Era Hill University (GEHU)]
- State private university established under the Uttarakhand State Legislature Act in 2011.
- Recognized by the UGC under Section 2(f) of the UGC Act.
- Programs aligned with national bodies:
  - AICTE (for technical programs)
  - BCI (for law programs)
  - PCI (for pharmacy programs, if offered)

[Common Recognitions and Partnerships]
- Both GEU and GEHU are registered on the AISHE (All India Survey on Higher Education) portal maintained by the Ministry of Education.
- Supported by the Department of Science & Technology (DST), Government of India, for a Technology Business Incubator (TBI).
- Industry-aligned partnerships with IBM, TCS, Adobe, Tata Technologies, and others for joint training, internships, and curriculum input.
- Academic tie-ups with international institutions for dual-degree programs, credit transfer, and student exchange opportunities.
